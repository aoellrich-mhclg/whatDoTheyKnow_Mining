Dear Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government,

Re your response to a previous request made through the whatdotheyknow.com website, which can be accessed here

https://www.whatdotheyknow.com/request/5...

On re- reading the correspondence supplied I have noticed reference to a telephone conversation for which no documentation has been supplied.

I am assuming that telephone calls are noted on your casework system and I would be grateful if you could therefore provide the notes of this and any other phone calls made or received.

As you have also not provided my own correspondence, I would be grateful if you could provide a printout of all communications logged on the system relating to 'Land South of Priory Road, Hungerford, West Berkshire. aka Land East of Salisbury Road, Hungerford.

Yours faithfully,

J A Giggins

Report
Despatch Box, Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government 17 July 2019

                            
Our reference: 4345117             Information request

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

 
Dear Sir/Madam
 
Environmental Information Regulations 2004
 
Thank you for contacting us on 16 July 2019 requesting: 
 
On re- reading the correspondence supplied I have noticed reference to a
telephone conversation for which no documentation has been supplied.

I am assuming that telephone calls are noted on your casework system and I
would be grateful if you could therefore provide the notes of this and any
other phone calls made or received.

As you have also not provided my own correspondence, I would be grateful
if you could provide a printout of all communications logged on the system
relating to 'Land South of Priory Road, Hungerford, West Berkshire. aka
Land East of Salisbury Road, Hungerford.
 
I am dealing with your request under the Environmental Information
Regulations 2004 and aim to send you a response by 14 August 2019 (EIR). 
The EIR offers the same parallel access to information as the Freedom of
Information Act.
 
If you have any questions, please ask by return email. Please leave the
subject line unchanged when replying, to make sure your email gets
straight to me.
 
Yours faithfully
 
 
MHCLG Knowledge & Information Access Team
 
 
 
 
NOTE: Please do not edit the subject line when replying to this email.
